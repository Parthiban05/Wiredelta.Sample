WELCOME TO MANUEL DUB

FESTIVAL VOLUNTEERS

Manuel Dub is a strictly not-for-profit event dedicated to the memory of Manuel. On the one hand, this has enabled us to

host some of dub music's finest artists who after feeling moved by the energy, dedication and love that goes into the event

agree to perform for a reduced fee or even free of charge. On the other hand it means that we are in constant need

of volunteers to make it happen and keep it going.
